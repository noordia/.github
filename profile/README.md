## What we do

You're handing real work to autonomous agents — in your CI, your pipelines, your products. It's powerful, and it's risky: an agent can quietly overspend, drift, or act in ways no one signed off on.

Noordia builds the **open-source tooling that keeps agentic AI safe, accountable and on-budget**, so you can adopt it without giving up control. Small, focused bricks you drop into the stack you already run — not a platform to migrate to.

## What you can count on

- 🛡️ **Safe by default** — budgets, gates and guardrails are the first feature, not an add-on.
- 🔍 **No black boxes** — open code, open governance, auditable behaviour.
- 🧱 **Drop-in, not lock-in** — composable tools that fit your existing CI and infra.
- 🆓 **Free and open** — permissively licensed, built to be used and forked.
